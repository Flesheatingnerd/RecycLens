About the Model

This model is trained to classify common types of recyclable plastics based on their visual appearance. Knowing the specific plastic type is crucial for proper recycling and disposal. Below is a quick guide to the plastics we classify:

Name Recycling # Common Uses Recyclability
**PET** 1 Beverage bottles, peanut butter jars, salad dressing bottles. Highly Recyclable
**HDPE** 2 Milk jugs, detergent bottles, shampoo bottles, rigid containers. Highly Recyclable
**LDPE** 4 Plastic bags, shrink wrap, squeezable bottles, flexible lids. Varies by facility
**PP** 5 Yogurt containers, medicine bottles, ketchup bottles, bottle caps. Good Recyclability
**PS** 6 Disposable cups, foam packaging (Styrofoam), CD cases. Rarely Recycled
**Other** 7 Mixed plastics, bioplastics, and multilayered materials. Not Recycled
